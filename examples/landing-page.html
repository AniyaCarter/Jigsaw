Jigsaw's Mission & Vision

Jigsaw’s mission is simple; helping employers match employees to roles they would be most successful in based upon data collected on different personality types, communication styles, and the work environment. It is the Jigsaw standard. Our vision is to foster a world in which smaller enterprises can competitively hire high quality staff.

Backstory
How did the idea for Jigsaw come about?

Growing up in the presence of a small business owner I have seen first hand the struggles and hardships that go into being a entrepreneur, the biggest one being finding quality employees that fit into the vision of the company, and through that the idea for Jigsaw was born. Jigsaw’s strives to connect employers to employees while giving, both parties, insight into how they fit into a workplace’s culture as well as what position is best suited for each individual based upon their personality type and communication style.
